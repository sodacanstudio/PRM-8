Entities

Below is a guide to the entities that we have discovered. We are trying to discover more to make better saftey precautions.

Entity 3 - Smilers

Known for their eerie glowing smiles in the dark, Smilers lurk in unlit areas and become hostile when provoked.

Entity :) - Partygoers

These unsettling entities are known to invite wanderers to "parties" and should be avoided at all costs.

Entity 9 - Facelings

Monke type entities with smooth, featureless faces. Generally non-hostile, though some are known to behave unpredictably.

Entity 10 - Skin-Stealers

These entities disguise themselves by wearing the skin of their victims. Approach with caution, as they are highly deceptive.
